Notes, Assumptions & Exclusions

Read carefully before relying on figures
  1. This is an estimate prepared from the Interior Designer's scope of works and selected supplier pricing.
  2. All rates are exclusive of Value Added Tax (VAT). VAT is shown separately at 15%.
  3. Base date is May 2026. Escalation beyond this date has not been allowed for and must be added by separate provision if works commence later.
  4. Preliminaries (Element 10.0) include Builders' Preliminaries (Supervision, Attendance, Transport, H&S) and QS Fee – Pre-Contract only.
  5. Provisional Sums (Element 9.0) carry a NIL allowance at this stage. To be confirmed once the scope is finalised.
  6. Excludes: Professional fees other than QS pre-contract, statutory approvals, occupational health & safety beyond builder allowance, contingencies, and any works not specified.
  7. Costs sourced from nominated suppliers: DF Strang (builders work – estimate), Weeks Interiors (ceilings), Vision Décor (furniture), Kingston (joinery), Afrigran (stonework), CW Electrical (electrical), and Tim Brooks (air conditioning).
  8. Issued via the Interior Designer for client review.
